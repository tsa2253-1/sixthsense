Why one sense at a time isn't enough

Most accessibility tools solve a single problem. A captioning app for the deaf. A screen reader for the blind. But real users live across multiple barriers, in unpredictable environments.

SixthSense combines all five tools under one roof, with a unified settings system, so users — and their families, teachers, or support workers — spend less time configuring and more time doing.
